About Me

This is me and what I do in my free time.

I would say I am a fairly interesting person. Most people have described me as fun which I wholeheartedly agree. I love going on all kinds of adventures, whether it be going grocery shopping or dancing in the moonlight.

My Family

I really love my family, they are so fun to be around! There are 9 of us total: Dad, Mom, Sam, me, Jared, Nate, Timmy, Marc, and Anna. The boys definitely outweigh the girls, but I would not have it any other way. My friends are always amazed when finding out how many siblings I have. And honestly, I think it is really funny.

My sister, Anna, is literally my pride and joy. I was the only girl for the longest time. I think I prayed every night for a sister until she came. Her and I are 13 1/2 years apart, which is a pretty big age gap! It is so fun having a little sister, she makes me so happy. However, it is pretty hard sometimes. For example, every time I come visit her, she has gotten so much older. I remember moving out and then seeing her only a couple months later, but she had learned to spell her own name when I was gone!

Overall, I love my family with all my heart and soul. They bring me so much joy.

Me

One of the things I do best is playing the guitar. I have been playing the acoustic for almost 8 years now. People always ask me what kind of music I play, but to be honest, I do not have one specific genre. Growing up, my dad introduced me to all kinds of music and it has stuck. I love listening to Weezer and Taylor Swift alike.

I also love to dance. I danced with the Southwest Dance Company for 11 years, and oh, how I miss it! Those were some of the best years I have experienced. Through dancing, I found my love of performancing. I am hoping someday soon I can enter a stage again.
